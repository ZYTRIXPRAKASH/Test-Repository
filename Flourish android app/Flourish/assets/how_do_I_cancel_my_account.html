How do I cancel my account?

Cancel your Flourish account

Step 1

From any page within the application bring down the down down menu next to your name at the top of the page. Within that menu click on "My Billing".

Step 2

On the "My Billing" page there is "Cancel Account" section on the right side of the screen. If you want to cancel your account simply click the button "I understand, please cancel my account". Please note that when you cancel your account all of your data associated with your account will be deleted.
