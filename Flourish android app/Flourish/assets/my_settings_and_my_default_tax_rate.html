How do I setup "My Settings" & "My default tax rate" ?

Anytime you are logged into the Flourish Application simply click your name in the top right hand corner of the page. This will bring down a drop down menu. From this menu select “My Settings”:

This will bring you to the “My Settings” page. Here you will want to enter all of your personal information:

After all of your personal information is enter you can set your default Tax Rate. To do this you will need to click on the “Invoice/Tax Settings” button:

Here you can enter your Tax Rate. Once the desired Tax Rate is entered hit the save button in the bottom right hand corner of the page:

After you have clicked “Save” your Default Tax Rate is set.
