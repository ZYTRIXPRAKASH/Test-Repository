How do I add discounts to and Invoice?

How to add discounts to an Invoice

Step 1

Anytime you're creating an invoice you have full control over discounting the price. Navigate down to the "Options" menu at the bottom of the page and enter the dollar amount that you would like to discount next to "Invoice Discount". Notice that you have the option of choosing to discount by dollar amount or percentage. The application will automatically adjust the total amount of the invoice for you.
