How do I create an inventory order?

Start by clicking on the “Inventory” button on the “Navigation Bar”:


This will take you to the “Inventory” page. From here you will want to click the “Inventory Orders” button on the top left side of the page:


You will now be taken to the “Orders” page. From here you will want to click the “Add Inventory Order” button on the top right side of the page:


This will bring up the “Add Order” window. You will want to name your order and click the “Save” button:


This will take you to the “Edit Order” page. Add the item(s) you are ordering. If they’re items not already listed in your inventory you will need to use the “Add New Product” button.
Once all items have been added to the order you can click the “Create Order” button. Or if the items have already been shipped to you you can click “Create Order and Mark Shipped” button:


Once you see the “Inventory Order” form you have successfully created and saved the order:
