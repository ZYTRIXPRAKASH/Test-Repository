How do I add a recurring appointment?

Start by clicking on the “Datebook” button on the “Navigation Bar”:


This will take you to the “Datebook” page. Heres where you can add Appointments, Bookings, and Tasks. To add a new Appointment click on the "Appointments" button at the top of the page:


You will now want to click on the "Add Appointment" button:


Here you can add in all the details about the appointment. At the bottom left portion of the details you have Repeat options Daily, Weekly, Monthly, and Yearly:
