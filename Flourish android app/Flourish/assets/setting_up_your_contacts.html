Setting up the your contacts

When you open the Contacts page you will be taken to an overview of all of your existing Contacts. To add a new Contact simply click the “Add Contact” button on the top right side of the page. You can easily sort your contacts using the buttons “Alphabetical”, “By Type”, and “By Status”. To Delete or Archive a contact check the box next to their name, and click the “Delete” or “Archive” button on the top left side of the Contact List.
