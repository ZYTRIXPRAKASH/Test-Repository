How do I add income not from sales?

Start by clicking on the “Money” button on the “Navigation Bar”:



This will take you to the “Expenses” page. Click the “Income” button on the top left side of the page to be taken to the “Income” page:


Once you’re on the “Income” page you can click the “Add Income” on the top right side of the page:


You will now see the “Add Income” window. The first thing you will want to do is add a new Income Account if you haven’t already done so. You can do this by clicking the button that says “New Income Type”:


Once you have created and selected the Income Account you can continue filling out the remaining fields. Once you have filled out all fields click the “Save” button in the bottom right corner:


Your income has now been added and saved.
