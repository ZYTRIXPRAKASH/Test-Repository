How to mark a product delivered

Once you create and save an Invoice products are not automatically marked as delivered. To mark them as “Delivered” you must first click on the “Deliveries” tab:


This will bring you to the “Deliveries” page. You can now select the Products you want to mark as delivered by checking the box next to the Products. Once you have selected all of the desired Products you can click the “Deliver Selected” button on the top right side of the page:


This will bring up the “Add Delivery” window. Here you have the chance to mark the items as hand delivered, or you may select a shipping service and enter in the tracking number for the product. When you are finished click the “Save” button:


You Products have now been successfully marked as delivered and your Inventory will automatically reflect these changes.
