How do I create a contact?

Start by clicking the “Contacts” button on the “Navigation Bar”:


This will take you to the “Contacts” page. Click on the “Add Contact” button on top right side of the page:


You will now see the “Add Contact” page. Fill in all the information you have about the contact and push the “Save” button on the bottom right side of the page:

TIP: If you want to add another contact click the “Save and add another” button.



When you see this page you have successfully added a contact:

TIP: Double check all of the contact information at this point. If you find any errors click the “Edit Contact” button on the top right side of the page.
