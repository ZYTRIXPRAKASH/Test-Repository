How do I add inventory?

Start by clicking on the “Inventory” button on the “Navigation Bar”:

This will take you to the “Inventory” page. Heres where you can add new inventory as well as view current inventory. To add inventory simply click the “Add New Product” button at the top right side of the page:

You will now be taken to the “Add Product” page. Fill in all information about the product. Once all information about the product is entered click the “Save” button on the bottom right side of the page:

TIP: You can add multiple products to your inventory by clicking the “Add Multiple Products” button on the top right side of the page.

Your product is now saved and added to your inventory. You can now view all the information about the added product:

TIP: Double check all of the product information at this point. If you find any errors click the “Edit Product” button on the top right side of the page.
