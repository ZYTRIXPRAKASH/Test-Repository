Getting started with Flourish

Step 1

This will be the first screen you see upon registering for your Flourish account. You'll want to to start by clicking the green "Get started with setting up your account!" button.

Step 2

Enter in all of your personal information and click "Save and Continue".

Step 3

Next we'll learn how to add in a contact. If you already have your contacts sorted on a Microsoft Excel Spreadsheet or a .CSV file you can use the "Import Contacts" feature. If not you'll have to enter in your contacts information using the fields below. When you're finished click "Save and Continue".

Step 4

Now it's time to add in your Products and Inventory. If you already have your Products sorted on a Microsoft Excel Spreadsheet or a .CSV file you can use the "Import Products" feature located under "More Instructions". If not you'll have to enter in your Product information using the fields below. When you're finished click "Save and Continue".

Step 5

Next we need to set all of our Sales and Order Settings. Here you'll have option to set up Sales Tax, Discounts, and more. Once you have everything set correctly click "Save and Continue".

Step 6

For your convenience we provide a brief tour video to show you around Flourish. Once you're done watching click "Continue".

Step 7

You're all done and ready to start using Flourish! If you have any questions you can use our "Live Chat" feature on the bottom right side of every page. Also don't forget to follow us on Facebook and Twitter!
