What is the Dashboard?

Dashboard:


The Dashboard will be the first page you see upon logging into your Flourish account:

 

Navigation Bar:

Located at the top of the page is the Navigation Bar. This will be what you use to navigate the Flourish Application. As you can see the “Dashboard” button is currently pushed meaning that we are currently on the Dashboard page.


-————————————————————————————————————————————————————

 

Shortcut Bar:

Located directly below the Navigation Bar on the top of the page is the Shortcut Bar. You have two menus on this bar, “Quick Add” and “Favorite Reports”:


Under the “Quick Add” menu you have instant access to adding an Invoice, Contact, Booking, Task, Expense, Income, and Mileage.


Under the “Favorite Reports” menu you will have instant access to your personal reports which you deem as favorites. This can be accomplished under the “Reports” button on the Navigation Bar.


-————————————————————————————————————————————————————

Personal Welcome:

Directly below the Shortcut Bar is your Personal Welcome. This will welcome you with your name, the current month and date, as well as a link to any incomplete tasks you may have added to your “Tasks” page.


-————————————————————————————————————————————————————

Trends:

Located directly below your Personal Welcome is the “Trends” window. Here you will get a graphical view of your Sales, Top Products, Bookings, and Profits and Losses, all for the calendar year. By default the “Trends” window will always start on the Sales graph. To switch between the four different graph categories simply click one of the four buttons located directly below the graph. These graphs update instantly as you manage your business through the Flourish application.


-————————————————————————————————————————————————————

Upcoming Events:

Located directly below the trends window is the “Upcoming Events” window. These are events that you have added to your personal “Datebook”. This is a stream of your upcoming activities. Your upcoming appointments, bookings, customer birthdays and anniversaries, and followups will display here to keep you up-to-date on whats to come.


-————————————————————————————————————————————————————

What’s New:

Located on the right side of the Dashboard is the “What’s New” window. This Window keeps you updated on the latest news and information from our team. Stay up to date with updates to the application as well as tips and industry news.


-————————————————————————————————————————————————————

Important Tasks:

Located on the right side of the Dashboard, directly below the “What’s New” window. These are high priority tasks that you have added on the “Tasks” page located in the “Datebook” section of the application.

TIP: You can mark a task as complete by checking the box to the right of the task.
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Useful Links:

Located on the right side of the Dashboard, directly below the “Important Tasks” window is the “Useful Links” window. These are helpful links with useful information for running your business.
